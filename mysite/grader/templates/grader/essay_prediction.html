  1. Select the specific essay prompt from the options.

  4. The model will pre-process the text and predict a score in the same rubric scale as the selected prompt.
  5. The predicted score will be displayed.

  • Minimum Score: Represents the minimum achievable score for a question.
  • Maximum Score: Represents the maximum achievable score for a question.
