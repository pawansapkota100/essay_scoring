About Us

Welcome to EssayMetrics! We are a community of students, educators, and essay enthusiasts who are passionate about writing improvement. Our goal is to provide tools and resources to help you get your best possible essay scores.

On our website, you can use our innovative essay scoring tool to get an instant predicted score for your essay based on key writing criteria. Our algorithm analyzes elements like thesis clarity, organization, evidence usage, and more to predict how an essay would fare on a standardized writing test. You can use this to refine and strengthen your writing before turning it in.

We also host a lively essay discussion forum where members can get feedback on essay drafts, ask questions about writing techniques, discuss current essay prompts or topics, and connect with other writers. In addition to our scoring tool and discussion forum, EssayMetrics provides writing tips.
